features · 03

The opposite of a finance app.

01

Numbers, not graphs

Every amount in JetBrains Mono, tabular-aligned, larger than the words around it. Balance figures are the hero of every screen.

02

The rule line

Borrowed from accounting ledgers. A 1.5px line under every section header and total. The structural backbone of the app.

03

The CHARA stamp

When a balance settles to zero, you get the stamp. Rotated two degrees. The only ALL CAPS in the entire system.

how it works · 03 steps

From shared dinner to settled in under a minute.

step 01

Start a group

Roommates, a trip, a one-time event. Add the people. That's it.

step 02

Add expenses

Amount first. Split equally, by share, or by exact amount. Receipts optional.

step 03

Settle

Chara computes the minimum number of transfers. Send via Swish. Done.

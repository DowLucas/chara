Cookie policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026

This site keeps cookies to the bare minimum. We don't run analytics scripts and we don't load third-party advertising tags. There's no cookie banner because there's nothing to consent to.

What we set on the marketing site (this page)

Nothing. The marketing site is fully static. No cookies, no localStorage, no tracking pixels.

What we set on the app (app.quits.app)

  • quits_session — HTTP-only, Secure, SameSite=Lax. Holds your signed session token. Strictly necessary. Cleared on sign-out.
  • quits_theme — your light/dark preference. Strictly necessary for UI. Stored in localStorage, never sent to the server.

Third parties

We use Cloudflare in front of the service. Cloudflare may set a __cf_bm cookie for bot mitigation, lasting up to 30 minutes. It contains no personal data.

That's the whole list. If you spot anything we missed, write to privacy@quits.app.

Data processing addendum

Last updated: 21 May 2026

This addendum applies when you use Quits in a way that makes you a data controller — for example, using a team or company account where your colleagues' personal data is processed. It supplements the terms of service.

1. Roles

You are the controller. Quits AB is the processor. We process personal data only on your documented instructions, which include the act of using the service as designed.

2. Subject matter and duration

We process personal data on your behalf for the duration of your account, plus the deletion windows specified in the privacy policy.

3. Nature and purpose

Storing, transmitting, and displaying expense data and attached receipts among the users you invite to your groups; sending notifications you trigger; supporting your account.

4. Categories of data

Names, email addresses, expense entries (amount, currency, date, description, group members), receipts you upload, and the operational logs described in the privacy policy. We do not request or expect special categories of data; if you upload them in receipts, that's your decision and your responsibility.

5. Sub-processors

The current list — Hetzner, Postmark, Expo / EAS, Cloudflare — is maintained in the privacy policy. We give 30 days' notice before adding a new sub-processor. You may object; if we can't resolve the objection, you may terminate the affected service.

6. Security measures

  • TLS 1.3 for all traffic.
  • Disk-level encryption for storage; encrypted backups.
  • Role-based access control internally; production access is logged and limited to on-call engineers.
  • Vulnerability monitoring on dependencies; security patches applied within their criticality SLA.
  • Incident response with notification to you within 72 hours of confirmed breach.

7. International transfers

Primary processing is in the EU. Sub-processors located outside the EU rely on Standard Contractual Clauses with supplementary measures as required under EU law and the EDPB's recommendations.

8. Assistance

We assist you, taking the nature of processing into account, with: responding to data subject requests; security obligations; breach notifications; DPIAs where applicable.

9. Audits

We make available the information needed to demonstrate compliance and allow reasonable audits on request, at your cost.

10. Deletion

On termination, we delete or return personal data per your instruction, within the windows in the privacy policy, unless retention is required by law.

11. Sign it

If you need a countersigned copy for your records, email privacy@quits.app.

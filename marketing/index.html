Questions, fairly answered.

If the answer isn't here, ask on GitHub. We reply.

Is Quits really free? +
Yes. The app is MIT licensed and every feature is available to every user. The hosted tier exists for people who don't want to run a server — it's priced to cover hosting, not to gate features.
How is this different from Splitwise? +
Splitwise added a daily expense limit on the free tier and bought-out features (multi-currency, receipts, charts) sit behind Pro. Quits keeps those open and adds the option to self-host. We also don't sell your data — there's nothing to sell.
Can I import my Splitwise history? +
Yes. Quits ships with a Splitwise CSV importer that maps groups, balances, and historical expenses. You won't lose anything in the move.
What about payments — does Quits move money? +
No. Quits tells you who owes who and how to make the smallest number of payments. Settlement happens in your existing payment app — Venmo, Swish, Wise, IBAN, whatever works. Less to break, less to regulate, less to leak.
Where is the hosted version run? +
EU-based infrastructure. GDPR-compliant by default. See the privacy policy for the data-processing details.
Can I export my data? +
Anytime, no questions, no waiting period. CSV for accounting, JSON for everything else. Delete-and-export are both one click.
